Our Products

Eye & Face Protection

Protecting vision is protecting life. ARA’ksha’s eye and face protection range safeguards against dust, impact, chemicals, heat, and airborne hazards. Designed for clarity, comfort, and compatibility with helmets and respirators, our solutions ensure workers stay protected, focused, and compliant in the toughest industrial environments.

Head Protection

Built to protect where it matters most. ARA’ksha’s head protection solutions guard against impact, falling objects, and workplace hazards across demanding industrial environments. Engineered for strength, comfort, and all-day wear, our safety helmets deliver reliable protection while ensuring compliance, confidence, and uninterrupted productivity on site.

Hand Protection

From precision tasks to heavy-duty handling, ARA’ksha’s hand protection range shields hands against cuts, abrasions, chemicals, heat, and impact—delivering grip, comfort, and confidence across every industrial application.

Foot Protection

Designed to stand up to the toughest conditions, ARA’ksha’s foot protection safeguards against impact, compression, slips, heat, and electrical hazards. Built for durability and all-day comfort, our safety footwear ensures stable footing, reliable protection, and confidence across every industrial environment.

Safety, Sanitation & Hygiene Workwear

ARA’ksha’s workwear is engineered to protect people while maintaining the highest standards of safety, sanitation, and hygiene. Designed for industrial, chemical, healthcare, and controlled environments, our garments provide barrier protection against dust, splashes, contaminants, and workplace hazards—while ensuring comfort, breathability, and ease of movement. Durable, compliant, and purpose-built, our workwear helps maintain clean operations, protects personnel, and supports safe, hygienic working conditions at all times.

Respiratory Protection (SCBA & PAPR)

When air quality becomes critical, ARA’ksha’s respiratory protection delivers uncompromised safety. Our range of SCBA, PAPR, and respirators protects against toxic gases, dust, fumes, and oxygen-deficient environments—ensuring reliable airflow, wearer comfort, and compliance in the most hazardous industrial and emergency conditions.
